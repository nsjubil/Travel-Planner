Jaipur, also known as the "Pink City," is a vibrant and culturally rich city in Rajasthan, India. It offers a perfect blend of historical monuments, royal palaces, bustling markets, and delectable cuisine. Here's a suggested itinerary to make the most of your trip to Jaipur:

Day 1: Arrival and City Tour

Arrive in Jaipur and check into your hotel.
After freshening up, start your city tour with a visit to the City Palace, a magnificent complex with courtyards, gardens, and impressive architecture.
Explore the nearby Jantar Mantar, an observatory with a collection of astronomical instruments.
Have lunch at a local restaurant and savor traditional Rajasthani dishes.
In the afternoon, visit the Hawa Mahal (Palace of Winds), an iconic pink sandstone structure with intricate windows and balconies.
Afterward, you can explore the vibrant markets of Jaipur, such as Johari Bazaar and Bapu Bazaar, to shop for souvenirs and handicrafts.
Return to your hotel in the evening and relax.

Day 2: Amber Fort and Jaigarh Fort

After breakfast, head to the majestic Amber Fort, located a short drive from the city center. Explore the fort's splendid architecture, beautiful palaces, and panoramic views of the surrounding hills.
You can opt to reach the fort on an elephant ride, subject to availability and your preference.
After visiting Amber Fort, drive to Jaigarh Fort, located on a hilltop and known for its massive cannon, Jaivana, which is one of the largest in the world.
Enjoy the views of the Aravalli hills and the city from Jaigarh Fort.
Return to the city and spend the evening at leisure. You can visit the Nahargarh Fort for a beautiful sunset view if you wish.

Day 3: Day Trip to Ajmer and Pushkar

Consider taking a day trip to Ajmer and Pushkar, two popular destinations located a few hours from Jaipur.
In Ajmer, visit the famous Ajmer Sharif Dargah, a significant Sufi shrine, and seek blessings.
Proceed to Pushkar, a holy town known for its sacred lake and the Brahma Temple.
Enjoy a walk around the colorful streets of Pushkar and visit the bustling Pushkar Market.
In the evening, return to Jaipur and spend the night at your hotel.

Day 4: Jaipur's Cultural Heritage

Start the day with a visit to the Albert Hall Museum, showcasing a diverse collection of art and artifacts from Rajasthan.
Explore the beautifully landscaped gardens of Sisodia Rani Ka Bagh, a royal garden built for the queen.
Have lunch at a heritage restaurant to experience traditional Rajasthani hospitality and cuisine.
In the evening, attend a cultural show at Chokhi Dhani, a village-themed resort, to experience Rajasthani folk music, dance performances, and a traditional dinner.
Return to your hotel for overnight stay.

Day 5: Departure

Depending on your departure time, you may have some free time for last-minute shopping or exploring a place you missed earlier.
Check out from your hotel and proceed to the airport or railway station for your onward journey, carrying with you wonderful memories of the Pink City, Jaipur.
Remember that this is a general itinerary, and you can customize it based on your interests and the duration of your stay. Jaipur has numerous attractions to offer, so take your time to soak in the royal heritage and vibrant culture of this enchanting city.
