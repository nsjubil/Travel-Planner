Kashmir, often referred to as "Paradise on Earth," is a breathtakingly beautiful region in the northern part of India. It is renowned for its stunning landscapes, snow-capped mountains, pristine lakes, and charming valleys. Here's a suggested itinerary for a memorable trip to Kashmir:

Day 1: Arrival in Srinagar

Arrive in Srinagar, the summer capital of Jammu and Kashmir, and check into a houseboat on Dal Lake or a hotel.
Spend the day acclimating to the beautiful surroundings and enjoying a shikara ride on Dal Lake to explore its floating gardens and markets.
In the evening, take a leisurely walk along the Boulevard Road, lined with Mughal gardens and enjoy the cool breeze of the lake.

Day 2: Srinagar Sightseeing

After breakfast, visit the famous Mughal Gardens - Nishat Bagh and Shalimar Bagh - both known for their well-manicured lawns, beautiful fountains, and stunning views of the Zabarwan Range.
Explore the historic Shankaracharya Temple, situated atop Shankaracharya Hill, which offers panoramic views of Srinagar city and Dal Lake.
In the afternoon, visit the enchanting Pari Mahal, also known as the "Palace of Fairies," which is a beautiful example of Islamic architecture.
Spend the evening at leisure, enjoying the serene ambiance of your houseboat or hotel.

Day 3: Gulmarg Excursion

Embark on a day trip to Gulmarg, often called the "Meadow of Flowers." The journey from Srinagar to Gulmarg is a scenic delight in itself.
Explore the lush green meadows of Gulmarg, which transform into a winter wonderland during the snowy season.
You can take a gondola ride (cable car) to Apharwat Peak for breathtaking views of the snow-capped peaks.
Enjoy optional activities like horse riding, golfing, or simply taking a leisurely walk through the beautiful landscape.
Return to Srinagar in the evening and relax.

Day 4: Pahalgam Excursion

Take another day trip to the charming town of Pahalgam, also known as the "Valley of Shepherds."
Enjoy the scenic drive along the Lidder River, passing through quaint villages and lush forests.
In Pahalgam, you can visit Betaab Valley, named after the Bollywood movie "Betaab," and Aru Valley, known for its picturesque surroundings and adventure activities.
If you're interested in leisurely walks, visit the Lidder River and enjoy the tranquility of the natural surroundings.
Return to Srinagar in the evening and spend your last night in the city.

Day 5: Departure from Srinagar

Depending on your departure time, you can spend your last morning in Srinagar shopping for souvenirs or enjoying a traditional Kashmiri meal.
Check out from your hotel or houseboat and proceed to the Srinagar airport for your onward journey, carrying with you cherished memories of the stunning landscapes and warm hospitality of Kashmir.

Please note that this is a general itinerary, and you can customize it based on your interests, the duration of your stay, and specific places you'd like to visit in Kashmir. Kashmir offers a plethora of natural beauty and recreational activities, ensuring an unforgettable experience in this paradise-like region.
