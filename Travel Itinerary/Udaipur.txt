Udaipur, also known as the "City of Lakes," is a beautiful and romantic city in Rajasthan, India, known for its stunning palaces, lakes, and rich cultural heritage. Here's a suggested itinerary to experience the best of Udaipur:

Day 1: Arrival in Udaipur and Sunset Boat Ride

Arrive in Udaipur and check into your hotel, preferably one with a view of Lake Pichola.
In the evening, head for a serene boat ride on Lake Pichola to witness the beautiful sunset over the lake and enjoy the views of the City Palace and other landmarks.
After the boat ride, you can explore the bustling streets of the old city and indulge in some shopping for traditional Rajasthani handicrafts and souvenirs.
Return to your hotel for an overnight stay.
Day 2: City Palace and Jag Mandir

After breakfast, visit the magnificent City Palace, a grand complex of palaces, courtyards, and gardens overlooking Lake Pichola.
Explore the various sections of the palace, including the Sheesh Mahal (Mirror Palace), Moti Mahal (Pearl Palace), and the museums displaying royal artifacts and memorabilia.
Afterward, take a boat ride to Jag Mandir, a beautiful island palace in the middle of Lake Pichola. Enjoy the serene surroundings and the impressive architecture of the palace.
Return to the city and have lunch at a rooftop restaurant with a view of the lake and the cityscape.
In the evening, you can opt for a cultural show with traditional music and dance performances at Bagore Ki Haveli or any other cultural center.
Overnight stay at your hotel.
Day 3: Explore the Old City

Start your day with a visit to the Saheliyon Ki Bari, a beautiful garden adorned with fountains, lotus pools, and marble pavilions. It was originally designed for the royal ladies to relax.
Next, visit the Fateh Sagar Lake, where you can take a boat ride or simply enjoy the view from the shore.
Head to the Sajjangarh Palace, also known as the Monsoon Palace, situated atop a hill. Enjoy the panoramic view of Udaipur and its lakes from this vantage point.
After lunch, visit the Jagdish Temple, an ancient Hindu temple known for its intricate carvings and architecture.
Spend the rest of the day exploring the narrow lanes and vibrant markets of the old city, such as Hathi Pol and Bada Bazaar.
Overnight stay at your hotel.
Day 4: Day Trip to Kumbhalgarh Fort and Ranakpur Jain Temple

Embark on a day trip to the majestic Kumbhalgarh Fort, located about 80 kilometers from Udaipur. This historic fortress offers stunning views of the Aravalli hills and has the second-longest wall in the world after the Great Wall of China.
On your way back to Udaipur, visit the renowned Ranakpur Jain Temple, famous for its intricate marble architecture and 1,444 exquisitely carved pillars.
Return to Udaipur in the evening and spend some leisure time.
Overnight stay at your hotel.
Day 5: Departure from Udaipur

Depending on your departure time, you may have some free time to relax or explore a place you missed earlier.
Check out from your hotel and proceed to the airport or railway station for your onward journey, carrying with you unforgettable memories of Udaipur's royal charm and picturesque landscapes.
Please note that this is a basic itinerary, and you can customize it according to your interests and the duration of your stay. Udaipur offers a wide range of experiences, from exploring historical palaces to enjoying the serene lakes and vibrant markets, ensuring a memorable and enchanting visit.
